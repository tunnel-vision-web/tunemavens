1. High-Level Vision & Ecosystem Strategy

The Intermaven Network Ecosystem is a unified, next-generation music-business operational platform and direct-to-consumer marketplace. Rather than forcing creators, record labels, music supervisors, and fans to fragment their workflows across multiple disconnected third-party software tools, our ecosystem combines AI business tools, direct catalog monetization, sync placement, and consumer streaming into a single, self-reinforcing network powered by a unified Single Sign-On (SSO) and shared credit engine.

4. Unique Market Positioning & Competitive Advantages
1. Closed-Loop Creator-to-Consumer Pipeline
Tracks ingested via TuneMavens flow instantly into SyncMavens for licensing pitches, TuneStream for consumer streaming, and Intermaven for AI social marketing.
2. Dual Licensing Frameworks & Exact Waterfall Breakdown (Option 1 & Option 2)
Unlike traditional sync publishers that lock up catalogs with advances, SyncMavens operates on two transparent frameworks:
  • Option 1 (Guaranteed Placements): 30% Agency Fee (off top) • 25% SyncMavens Admin Fee of remainder (17.5% gross) • 52.5% Net Creator Royalty Pool • 100% Writer & Composer Publishing Shares Retained.
  • Option 2 (Sync Brief AI & 'Sync Ready' Gallery): 15% Agency Fee paid to SyncMavens • 85% Direct Net Creator Payout • AI brief matching, 45s previews, stem vault.
  • $0 Upfront Advances: Zero predatory catalog buyouts. We only earn when creators get placed.
3. Mother-CMS & Shared Token Architecture
Intermaven acts as the mother platform driving shared design tokens, multi-tenant UI grids, and localized key overlays across all sub-applications.
4. Native AI Business & Social Studio
Built-in AI tools (Claude Sonnet 4.6 Recommendation Agent, Gemini/Sora Social AI Studio) generate channel-optimized creative assets (1:1 square, 9:16 vertical video).
5. Shared Single Sign-On (SSO) & Unified Credit Bank
Sign in once across all 4 platforms. Credits earned from TuneStream listener tips or marketplace sales are spent on AI runs or CRM marketing campaigns.
