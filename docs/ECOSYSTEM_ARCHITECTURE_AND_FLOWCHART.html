2. End-to-End Data & Asset Lifecycle Sequence
  1. Catalog Ingestion: Creator uploads audio stems to TuneMavens. Stems store in AWS S3, metadata upserts to MongoDB catalogs with auto-generated ISRCs.
  2. Split Cascade Lock: Creator defines split waterfall (90/10) in publishing_deals and signs contract via contracts.
  3. Sync Placement: Music supervisor posts brief on SyncMavens. AI Match Simulator evaluates track compatibility. Pitch delivered with watermarked preview. Supervisor approves and 90/10 royalty cascade executes.
  4. Consumer Streaming: Track publishes to TuneStream. Fans stream lossless audio & tip artist directly. Revenue credits route to unified network balance.
  5. Social AI Marketing: Creator triggers Intermaven Social AI Studio to render 1:1 and 9:16 creative assets (Gemini/Sora) and dispatch via Resend email / social DM.
